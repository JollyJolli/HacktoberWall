About HacktoberWall

HacktoberWall is an open-source initiative that encourages newcomers to contribute to open source projects. Our annual archive showcases contributor names, each year marked with a unique design and visual style.

Founded by Jolly (Owner) and Phanty (Co-owner), HacktoberWall remains committed to promoting inclusivity and community in open source, especially during the spooky season of Hacktoberfest.
